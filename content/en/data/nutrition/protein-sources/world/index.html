Data notes:

FAO historical data to 2013 uses old methodology while data from 2010 to present uses new methodology. Proteindata.org has merged the two data sets and dropped the overlapping observations (2010-2013) from the historical data in favor of the newer methodology. OurWorldInData drops overlapping observations in favor of old methodology.
